About Us

At Camp Embark, we believe every child deserves a safe opportunity to use their minds and creative energy to explore, create new friendships, and embark upon learning experiences that spark their curiosity and excitement. We make learning FUN!

Our Value Proposition

No child leaves our care the same way they arrived. Their lives are changed. Their mind and spirit are elevated and a new passion for life is ignited.

Our Mission

Our mission aligns with our belief that empowered children are confident in their purpose, passions, and abilities. Their future contributions in the home, school, and in business, foster community transformation.

SETTING THE STAGE

We recognize the value and necessity of play as a foundation for learning. Camp Embark is safe ground for our youth to embark on a journey filled with awesome discovery about nature, science, leadership, and self-empowering life skills like financial literacy. Fun is the foundation on which all of our programs are developed. Experienced teachers and counselors instruct our children in the fundamentals of leadership, self-awareness, cultural value, ownership, and relationship development all while having…FUN! While many day camps have traditional activities, we offer a well-rounded experience that considers the needs and interests of children who often feel excluded or odd engaging in traditional camp activities. Ingrained within our curriculum is an emphasis on cultural appreciation and celebration. We want our children to see reflections of themselves within our activities and within our camp counselors. This is necessary as we seek to gain the trust of every child and parent that enters our day camp. When children feel they belong and that their identity and voice matters, learning becomes FUN!

PROFESSIONAL STAFF & INFLUENCERS

The value and strength of our curriculum are only as strong as the value of our staff. Our counselors and teachers are vetted using the highest standards in background checking. We maintain a staff of counselors and teachers who are respected in their communities and in the marketplace. Their engagement is a labor of love not of duty.

We invite community leaders and key influencers to share their journeys of success with our children. When children see successful individuals who look like them, their belief in their potential for success soars beyond all societal or self-limiting beliefs.

PARTNERS WITH PARENTS

Camp Embark is a nurturing environment for children ages 5 – 12. We cater to parents who want to enrich their children’s lives and prepare them for real-world leadership. We partner with parents who understand the value of investing in their child’s development. We diligently strive to keep the lines of communication open with our parents to ensure the very best experience for their children. Together, we empower our little leaders to become the great leaders of tomorrow. We agree with the age-old adage: It does take a village to raise a child.
