Our Program

Camp Embark takes a holistic approach to create a schedule conducive for learning and FUN!

Camp Embark takes a holistic approach to create a schedule conducive for learning and FUN! We believe creative expression is often the easiest avenue to engage even the shyest of youth. Our menu of activities is designed to strengthen youth in every area of their development.

Each activity is age-appropriate and is designed to stir curiosity and to keep our children asking, how? Why? When? Where and even Why Not? We are shaping innovators and leaders and leaders ask questions before they ever start giving answers.
